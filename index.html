I'm Kai, an Oakland-based full-stack web developer with a strong emphasis in RoR, currently seeking a Junior Web Developer position. I recently graduated from General Assembly's Web Development Immersive. Before WDI, I was designing & defining processes and standards for the quality control team at Minted, wrangling Google Spreadsheets into little project management tools. I've always been the person people come to for answers, because I'm always curious enough to go find them. That curiosity is a big part of what lead me to WDI, and what makes me so happy to be coding every day.
